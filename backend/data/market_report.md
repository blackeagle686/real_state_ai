# Real Estate Market Report 2026 - Saudi Arabia

## Overview
The real estate market in Saudi Arabia is witnessing significant growth, driven by Vision 2030 initiatives. Riyadh remains the primary hub for commercial and residential investments.

## Price Trends
- **Riyadh**: Prices for land have increased by 15% year-on-year. Apartments in North Riyadh are highly sought after.
- **Jeddah**: Stability in prices with a slight increase in luxury waterfront properties.
- **Dammam**: Emerging market with high potential for industrial and residential development.

## Ranking of Cities for Investment
1. **Riyadh**: Best for capital appreciation.
2. **Jeddah**: Best for lifestyle and tourism-related real estate.
3. **Makkah & Madinah**: Best for long-term rental yields during Hajj and Umrah seasons.

## Recommendations
For families: Look for apartments in Dammam or Riyadh suburbs for better value.
For investors: High-end commercial land in Jeddah King Road is currently top-ranked.
